ABOUT THE SHOW

Wild Hearted Friends, hosted by childhood friends Vic and Erin, is a podcast that brings people together through honest conversation, shared curiosity, and meaningful connection. Each episode explores the stories, ideas, and experiences that shape who we are, reminding us that even in a fast-moving world, authentic dialogue still has the power to unite us. With warmth, humor, and an open mind, Wild Hearted Friends invites listeners to reflect, grow, and embrace what it means to live with a wild heart.
